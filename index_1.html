About Hicham

My Background

As a graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematical and Informatical Sciences, I possess a robust foundation in analytical thinking and logical reasoning. Throughout my university years, I delved into the complexities of mathematics and computer science, mastering concepts in algebra, calculus, and probability theory. This academic journey not only cultivated my problem-solving skills but also ignited a passion for leveraging technology to address real-world challenges.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I am deeply passionate about exploring diverse interests and hobbies. From immersing myself in the world of literature to experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, I believe in leading a well-rounded and fulfilling life.
